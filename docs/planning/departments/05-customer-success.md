# departments/05 — Customer Success

> The retention/expansion persona: monitor customer health, spot expansion whitespace, and drive renewals
> with fresh contact data. Framework: [25](../25-departments-teams-workspaces.md).

## 1. Purpose & users
CSMs protect and grow existing customers — health, expansion, renewals. **Users:** CSMs + CS managers.
`department_type = customer_success`.

## 2. Persona & surfaces (6 destinations)
- **Home** → CS health dashboard (§3). **Prospect** → expansion whitespace within customer accounts +
  buying-committee refresh (`24`). **Sequences/Inbox** → lifecycle outreach + replies. **Reports** →
  retention/expansion pack.

## 3. Dashboard & KPIs
Customer health score, renewal pipeline, expansion whitespace ($), champion job-change alerts, NRR/GRR
inputs, contact freshness on customer accounts (`22`), at-risk accounts.

## 4. Workflows & automations (`27`)
- **Champion-change alert**: job-change signal on a key contact → notify CSM + suggest re-engagement.
- **Renewal play**: N days pre-renewal → task + brief (AI summary, `23`).
- **Expansion signal**: new-hire/tech-install in a customer → whitespace play.
- **Health-drop**: risk threshold → escalation task.

## 5. Permissions, visibility & budgets
CSMs: reveal/refresh on owned customer accounts; managers: portfolio views. Customer records often
`visibility=team` (`H18`). Reveal/refresh spend under **per-team budget**.

## 6. Reporting
Renewal forecast, expansion attribution, health trends, champion-coverage/freshness; visibility-scoped.

## 7. Collaboration
Handoff from Sales on close, shared account context, escalation to Support (`06`), QBR prep.
