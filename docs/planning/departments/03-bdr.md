# departments/03 — BDR

> The outbound new-business persona: net-new account discovery, multi-threaded outbound, and pipeline
> creation. Framework: [25](../25-departments-teams-workspaces.md).

## 1. Purpose & users
Business Development Reps focus on **net-new** outbound — discovering and opening target accounts.
**Users:** BDRs (persona) + BDR managers. `department_type = bdr`. (Distinct from SDR's inbound emphasis.)

## 2. Persona & surfaces (6 destinations)
- **Home** → BDR pipeline-creation dashboard (§3). **Prospect** → net-new ICP/whitespace segments,
  technographic + intent filters (`24`). **Sequences/Inbox** → outbound + replies. **Reports** → sourced-
  pipeline pack.

## 3. Dashboard & KPIs
New accounts opened, accounts touched, multi-thread depth (contacts/account), meetings sourced, sourced
pipeline $, reveal efficiency, credit burn vs. **team budget**.

## 4. Workflows & automations (`27`)
- **Whitespace discovery**: AI/segment surfaces untouched ICP accounts → assign to BDR.
- **Multi-threading**: on account open, suggest + (approve) reveal the buying committee (`23`).
- **Intent triggers**: tech-install / hiring signal on a target account → play.
- **Handoff**: sourced opportunity → AE (Sales, `01`) with context.

## 5. Permissions, visibility & budgets
BDRs: reveal/sequence on assigned target accounts + workspace-visible; managers: territory assignment +
team views. **Per-team budget** governs reveal spend.

## 6. Reporting
Sourced-pipeline attribution, account-coverage, outbound funnel, territory penetration; visibility-scoped.

## 7. Collaboration
Territory planning with managers, shared target-account lists/segments, handoff to Sales, coaching.
